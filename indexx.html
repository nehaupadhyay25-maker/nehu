🍜 Maggi Recipe (Maggi Love ❤️)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1½ cups water
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 green chili (optional 🌶️)
  • Maggi taste-maker (from the pack)

Steps:

  1. Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, and chili → sauté for 2 minutes.
  3. Add 1½ cups of water and let it boil.
  4. Break the Maggi noodles and add them to the pan.
  5. Add the Maggi taste-maker.
  6. Cook for 2–3 minutes until the noodles turn soft.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggi! 🍜

"❤️Samosa Love❤️"

❤️"Golden Delight Samosas"❤️

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas.
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder.
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala.
  • Salt to taste.
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste.
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice.
  • 1 pack of samosa pastry sheets.
  • Oil for frying.

Steps

  1. Prepare filling: Heat oil, sauté ginger-garlic paste, add potatoes, peas, spices, lemon juice.
  2. Shape samosas: Fill pastry sheets, fold into triangle.
  3. Fry: Deep fry until golden & crispy.
  4. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

"❤️Sizzling Aloo Paratha Bliss"

🌾 Desi Delight Paratha🩷

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour (atta).
  • Water (as needed).
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee.
  • Salt to taste.

Steps

  • Make dough: Mix flour, salt, and water to form a smooth dough. Rest it.
  • Prepare filling: Mash potatoes, add spices & coriander.
  • Assemble: Roll dough, stuff with filling, roll again.
  • Cook: Roast on tawa with oil/ghee until golden.
  • Serve hot with yogurt or pickle. 😊
