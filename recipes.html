🍜 Maggi Recipe (Maggi Love ❤️)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1½ cups water
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 green chili (optional 🌶️)
  • Maggi taste-maker (from the pack)

    Steps:

  1. Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a pan.
    2. Add chopped onion, tomato, and chili → sauté for 2 minutes.
  2. Add 1½ cups of water and let it boil.
  3. Break the Maggi noodles and add them to the pan.
  4. Add the Maggi taste-maker.
  5. Cook for 2–3 minutes until the noodles turn soft.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggi! 🍜

"Golden Delight Samosas"

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas.
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder.
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala.
  • Salt to taste.
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste.
  • 1tbsp lemon juice.
  • 1 pack of store-bought samosa pastry sheets.
  • Oil for frying.
  1. Prepare the filling:
  • Heat oil in a pan, sauté ginger-garlic paste for 1 minute.
  • Add boiled mashed potatoes, peas, cumin powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Stir in lemon juice and let the filling cool.
  1. Shape the samosas:
  • Take a samosa pastry sheet, fold into a cone, and seal with water.
  • Fill with the potato mixture and seal the top to form a triangle.
  1. Fry the samosas:
  • Heat oil in a pan and fry the samosas until golden and crispy (3-4 minutes).
  1. Serve:
  • Serve hot with chutney or ketchup. Enjoy!

Desi Delight Paratha

Ingredients

  1. For the dough.
  • 2 cups wheat flour (atta).
  • Water (as needed).
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee.
  • Salt to taste.
  1. Steps
  • Make dough: Mix flour, salt, and water to form a smooth dough. Let it rest.
  • Prepare filling: Mash potatoes, add cumin, chili powder, salt, and coriander.
  • Assemble: Roll dough into small balls, stuff with filling, and roll out into a paratha.
  • Cook: Cook on a hot tawa, applying oil or ghee, until golden on both sides.
  • Serve with yogurt or pickle. Enjoy! 😊
